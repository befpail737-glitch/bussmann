Complete protection strategies for variable frequency drives and motor drive systems using authentic Bussmann products.

VFD Protection Overview

Variable frequency drives (VFDs) are critical components in industrial automation and process control systems. Protecting these sophisticated power electronics requires specialized circuit protection that can withstand motor-starting inrush while providing rapid protection against destructive short circuits.

Application Challenges

  • High motor-starting currents that can be 6-10 times full load current
  • Sensitive semiconductor devices (IGBTs, diodes) requiring precise protection
  • Need for coordination between input and output protection
  • Harsh industrial environments with voltage transients
  • Continuous operation requirements in critical processes

Bussmann Solution

The Bussmann 170M series high-speed fuses provide the ideal solution for VFD protection applications. These current-limiting, fast-acting fuses feature precise I²t characteristics that coordinate with the VFD's short-circuit withstand capability.

Recommended Protection Strategy

Input Protection

170M series fuses sized according to VFD manufacturer recommendations for input protection against line-side faults.

DC Link Protection

Specialized fuses for DC link protection in case of internal VFD faults.

Output Protection

Appropriate protection between VFD and motor as recommended by VFD manufacturer.
