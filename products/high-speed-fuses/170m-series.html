Bussmann 170M Series High-Speed Fuses

Industry-leading high-speed fuses for VFDs and UPS applications. Features precise I²t characteristics for semiconductor protection.

170M Series Overview

The Bussmann 170M series represents the industry standard for high-speed fuses used in variable frequency drive (VFD) and uninterruptible power supply (UPS) applications. These current-limiting, fast-acting fuses are specifically designed to protect sensitive semiconductor devices from destructive overcurrents.

Key Features

  • Precise I²t characteristics for proper coordination with semiconductors
  • Current-limiting design reduces fault energy let-through
  • Fast operation typically faster than 1/2 cycle
  • Wide voltage and current range coverage
  • Industry standard dimensions for common mounting

Applications

  • Variable Frequency Drives (VFDs)
  • Uninterruptible Power Supplies (UPS)
  • DC Link Protection
  • Semiconductor Equipment Protection
  • Power Electronics

Engineer's Perspective

When implementing 170M series fuses for VFD protection, it's critical to ensure proper coordination with the drive's short circuit withstand capability. The precise I²t characteristics of these fuses make them ideal for protecting the sensitive IGBTs and diodes in modern power electronics.

Our application engineers recommend selecting the fuse based on the VFD's input current rating and the available fault current at the installation point. For detailed coordination studies, contact our technical support team.
