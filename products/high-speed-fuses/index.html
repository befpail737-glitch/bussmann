Bussmann High-Speed Fuses

Fast-acting protection for semiconductor devices with precise I²t characteristics. Ideal for protecting sensitive electronics in harsh environments.

High-Speed Fuse Categories

170M Series

Industry-leading high-speed fuses for VFDs and UPS applications. Features precise I²t characteristics for semiconductor protection.

The 170M series provides reliable protection for variable frequency drives, UPS systems, and other power electronics applications.

170M Series Details

FWP Series

Fast-acting fuses for DC applications. Designed for battery protection and energy storage systems.

FWP series fuses offer high performance in DC protection applications with excellent interrupting ratings.

FWP Series Details

Low-Peak (LPJ/LPS) Series

Current-limiting fuses designed for motor protection. Provides excellent motor-starting capability.

LPJ and LPS series fuses are ideal for motor protection applications where high motor-starting currents are expected.

Applications for High-Speed Fuses

VFD Protection

Protect variable frequency drives from semiconductor damage with precisely coordinated fuses.

UPS Systems

Ensure reliable operation of uninterruptible power supplies with appropriate fuse protection.

Semiconductor Protection

Preserve valuable electronics with fuses specifically designed for sensitive components.

Power Electronics

Protect rectifiers, inverters, and other power electronics from destructive overcurrents.

Technical Overview

I²t Coordination

High-speed fuses offer precise I²t characteristics, allowing proper coordination with upstream and downstream devices.

Fast Operation

These fuses operate faster than the half-cycle point, limiting the let-through energy to protect sensitive components.

Current Limiting

Current-limiting design significantly reduces fault current magnitude and energy let-through.
